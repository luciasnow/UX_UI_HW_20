Case Study: Prairie Protection Colorado

This case study goes into detail around my 3 week group project that took place in 2023 redesigning the website of Prairie Protection Colorado, a local nonprofit in Colorado dedicating to the prairie wildlife of the Front Range.
Prairie Protection Colorado (PPC) is a unique organization with a multitude of offerings for users. For this project, we focused on a user who is looking to save their own local colony and pursuing PPC in order to gain support in their cause.

Team: Rachel Brayton, Christina Pham, & Lucy Snow

Research
